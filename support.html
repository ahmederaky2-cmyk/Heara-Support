Heara Support

Support for Heara 1.0 · Train your musical ear

Contact

For help, feedback, or accessibility questions, email eraky@gmx.de. Please include your iPhone or iPad model, iOS version, and a short description of what happened. Do not send passwords or sensitive personal information.

Audio troubleshooting

  • Turn up media volume and check the device's audio settings.
  • Check whether sound is routed to Bluetooth headphones, a speaker, or another connected device.
  • Leave the lesson, return to it, and try playback again.
  • If audio remains unavailable, fully close and reopen Heara.

Progress and data

Heara stores progress locally and does not require an account. To reset local learning history and preferences, use the reset option in Heara's Settings. This action cannot be undone.

Accessibility

Heara supports VoiceOver, Dynamic Type, Dark Mode, Increased Contrast, and Reduced Motion. If an exercise or control is difficult to use with an accessibility setting, contact us with the lesson name and device configuration.

Privacy

Read the Heara Privacy Policy. Heara does not collect personal data, show ads, use tracking, or upload practice audio.

Acknowledgements

Third-party and content acknowledgements included with the app are available from Profile → Settings → Acknowledgements.
