Privacy Policy

Effective 20 July 2026 · Heara version 1.0

Heara is designed to work offline and to keep your learning activity private on your device.

Data collection

Heara does not collect personal data. The app has no account system, advertising, analytics SDK, tracking SDK, social features, or remote backend. Heara does not request microphone access and does not upload audio.

Data stored on your device

Exercise attempts, lesson progress, mastery and scaffolding state, preferences, XP, achievements, and streak information are stored locally on your device using Apple system storage. Learning recommendations and progress summaries are calculated on the device and are not sent to the developer or third parties.

Deleting local data

You can remove Heara's local practice history and preferences from the app's Settings. Deleting the app also removes its locally stored data, subject to normal iOS backup behavior controlled by your Apple settings.

Third parties and tracking

Heara does not sell or share personal information and does not track you across apps or websites. The production app contains no third-party analytics or advertising packages.

Children

Because Heara does not collect personal data or provide accounts, messaging, advertising, or user-generated content, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes

If Heara later adds online services or changes its data practices, this policy will be updated before those changes are released.

Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to eraky@gmx.de.
